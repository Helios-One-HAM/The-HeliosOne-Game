My HAM callsign is KD2GVE. Here is my submission for the grassroots campaign.

My idea for the Ham radio campaign is simple.
Let's pick a theme like pirates and have ham radio operators become "Pirate Captains" to "recruit a crew" of non hams.

Those that are responsible enough to sail the High Seas of the EMF Waves.
Those who are called "Pirates" have to follow the "Pirate's Code" — a rebranding of the FCC operator rules to make them more appealing to the next generation of kids.
We can add extra harmless fun rules and "pirate" games too. Like "Pirates have to say ARRRRR!!!" and singing sea shanties.

If anyone doesn't understand how to act the part of a "Pirate" just point them to the One Piece kid's cartoon and tell them to watch what the pirates do in order to have Fun.
The show is all about having fun.

The bottom line is: don't cause any abuse and have fun!

Hams can make up whatever fun silly rules they like about being a pirate,
but the most important thing about a pirate is his freedom. It is a special thing and its dangerous. Hams know it all too well.
So I think this theming is fun and motivating for the kids.
Hams are free to come up with whatever fun ideas they can.


This is the "Davy Jones rules template" for other HAMs to use to be "pirates."

All Ham Pirates need a "Pirate Code" to follow in order to be pirates.
There should be some extra ground rules we all agree on before starting this show.
- All Pirate crews must have a Captain that is a HAM.
- All Pirates must have fun and remain lighthearted, away from the worries of the world. We want anyone to feel like they can be a ham pirate.
- Pirates should try to dress up.
- For anyone to become a Pirate Captain, they must be willing to sign their name in Davy Jones' locker. (Becoming a Ham) 
  And then make a proclimation to the world and davy jones.
- Pirates must attempt to "plunder" each other (share QSL cards).

Any captain may make any rule for his crew to follow, provided it doesn't break FCC guidelines or cause abuse or harm to anyone.
