My HAM callsign is KD2GVE. Here is my submission for the grass roots campaign.

My idea for the Ham radio campaign is simple.
Lets pick a theme like pirates and have ham radio operators become "Pirate captains"

That are responsible enough to sail the High Seas of the EMF Waves.
Those who are called "Pirates" Have to follow the "Pirate's Code" or a rebranding of the FCC operators rules to make them more appealing to the next generation of kids.
We can add extra harmless fun rules and "pirate" games too. Like "Pirates have to say ARRRRR!!!"

If anyone doesn't understand how to act the part of a "Pirate" just point them to the One Piece kid's cartoon to give em the premise.
The bottom line is don't cause any abuse and have fun!

Hams can make up whatever fun silly rules they like about being a pirate.
But the most important thing about a pirate is his freedom. It is a special thing and is dangerous. Hams know it all too well.
So I think this theming is fun and motivating for the kids.
Hams are freed to come up with whatever fun ideas they can.


This is the "Davey Jones template" for other HAM's to use to be "pirates".

All Pirates need "Pirate Rules" to follow in order to be pirates. 
There should be some extra ground rules we all agree on before starting this show.
- All Pirate crews must have a Capitain that is a HAM.
- All Crews must sail the seas frequently. No Hiding like a Land lubber.
- All Pirates must have fun and remain lighthearted away from the worries of the world.
- Pirates should try to dress up and use "jolly roger" type immagry.
- For anyone to become a Pirate they must be willing to sign their name in Davy Jone's locker.

Any captain make make any rule for his crew to follow, provided it doesn't cause abuse or harm to anyone.
